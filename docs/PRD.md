# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Project Overview
**Product Name:** Websuem Web Platform
**Objective:** To build a comprehensive web application for the Websuem agency that serves as a premium marketing landing page, a secure client portal, and a powerful internal CRM.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Prospective Clients:** Startups and growth-oriented businesses looking for high-quality, fast-turnaround web and AI automation services.
*   **Active Clients:** Existing customers needing visibility into their ongoing projects, invoices, and deliverables.
*   **Internal Team (Admin):** Agency operators needing to track leads, manage projects, and oversee business operations.

## 3. Core Features & Scope

### 3.1 Marketing Landing Page
*   **Hero Section:** High-conversion messaging emphasizing "Agency-quality results, built in days". Strong Call to Action (CTA).
*   **Services Page:** Detailed breakdowns of Web Development, AI Automation, Brand Identity, and Social Media Management.
*   **About Us:** Information about the solo-founder/next-gen agency model, emphasizing direct access and transparency.
*   **Contact Us:** Lead generation form connected directly to the internal CRM.

### 3.2 Client Portal
*   **Authentication:** Secure login via email/password or Magic Link.
*   **Dashboard:** Overview of active projects and general status.
*   **Project Details & Timeline:** Visual tracking of the 6-step workflow (Discovery → Proposal → Design → Build → Review → Launch).
*   **Invoicing & Billing:** View, download, and track the status of invoices.
*   **Asset Hub:** Secure area for sharing deliverables and resources.

### 3.3 Internal CRM (Admin Dashboard)
*   **Lead Management:** Kanban or list view of incoming leads from the landing page.
*   **Client Management:** Database of all active and past clients.
*   **Project Tracker:** Manage active projects, update timelines (which reflect on the client portal).
*   **Financial Dashboard:** Overview of generated invoices and revenue tracking.

## 4. Success Metrics
*   Conversion rate on the landing page contact form.
*   Zero manual status update emails (handled via Client Portal).
*   Seamless management of leads through the CRM.
