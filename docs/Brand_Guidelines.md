# Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity
**Websuem** is a modern, AI-powered web design and development agency.
*   **Voice:** Professional, transparent, efficient, and forward-thinking.
*   **Key Messaging:** "Agency-quality results, built in days." "Next-gen agency model."
*   **Vibe:** Premium, sleek, reliable, and highly technical.

## 2. Color Palette
To reflect a modern tech/AI aesthetic:
*   **Background (Primary):** Rich Dark (e.g., `#0A0A0A` or `#111111`) to give a premium software feel.
*   **Surface/Cards:** Slightly lighter darks (e.g., `#1A1A1A`) with glassmorphic overlays.
*   **Accent (Primary):** A striking, futuristic color like Electric Blue (`#2563EB`) or a vibrant Purple/Pink gradient to signify AI and speed.
*   **Text (Primary):** Off-white (`#F3F4F6`) for high readability on dark backgrounds.
*   **Text (Secondary):** Muted Gray (`#9CA3AF`) for subtext and less critical information.

## 3. Typography
*   **Font Family:** `Inter`, `Geist`, or `Plus Jakarta Sans`. These provide a clean, geometric, and highly readable look suited for software and agency sites.
*   **Headings:** Bold, tightly kerned.
*   **Body:** Regular weight, generous line height (e.g., `leading-relaxed`).

## 4. Visual Assets
*   Use abstract geometric shapes, subtle gradients, and high-quality mockups to showcase previous work.
*   Avoid standard stock photos; prefer UI snippets, code blocks, or custom 3D renders.
